Enhanced Acknowledgement of Country

An Acknowledgement of Country is an important practice in Australia that recognises the Traditional Custodians of the land and their enduring connection to Country.

Guidance for writing a meaningful acknowledgement:
  • Research the Traditional Custodians of the specific area
  • Be specific about the Country and peoples you are acknowledging
  • Avoid tokenistic or generic statements
  • Speak with sincerity and respect
